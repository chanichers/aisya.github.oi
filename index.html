My Personal Writing

I feel that life is one of the most beautiful gifts for us to live that is bestowed by Allah swt. As we experience the beauty of the world, let us not forget the duty we have to do as a servant of Allah swt. Surely we will feel the sweetness of this life if we are able to balance the things of this world and the things of the hereafter. We are also certainly not spared from making mistakes because we are just a humble servant of Allah. Believe me, every repentance that is done will be blessed with goodness instead. Always believe that Allah is testing us because He knows we are capable of surviving it.

To those who are in trouble, never get tired of asking for help and pray to Allah swt, because He is All -Hearing and All -Merciful. Assume that each such test is a grace and as a sign of Allah's love and affection for us.
